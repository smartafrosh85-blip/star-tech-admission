About Us

School🏢 History

Established in 2024, Star Tech has been a beacon of excellence in education in the upper west region. Founded by a group of visionary educators, the school has grown from humble beginnings to a reputable institution with a strong commitment to academic excellence and character development. Over the years, we've nurtured generations of leaders, innovators, and change-makers who have gone on to make a positive impact in their communities.

Mission

At Star Tech, our mission is to provide holistic education that empowers students to reach their full potential. We strive to create a supportive and inclusive learning environment that supports academic excellence, creativity, and critical thinking. Our goal is to equip students with the knowledge, skills, and values necessary to succeed in an ever-changing world.

Vision

Our vision is to be a leading school in the region and beyond, renowned for our commitment to academic excellence, innovation, and community service. We aspire to inspire a love of learning, promote social responsibility, and develop well-rounded individuals who are equipped to make a positive difference in the world.

Admissions

At Star Tech, we welcome students of diverse backgrounds who share our values of academic excellence, innovation, and community service. We offer admission to students in various programs including KG1-KG3, JHS, and International Baccalaureate.

Admission Requirements

  • Completed application form
  • Birth certificate
  • Academic transcripts or report cards (for continuing students)
  • Entrance exam results (if applicable)
  • Two letters of recommendation (for continuing students)
  • Proof of residence

Application Process

  1. Submit your application form and required documents
  2. Pay the application fee
  3. Take an entrance exam (if required)
  4. Attend an interview (if required)
  5. Receive admission decision
